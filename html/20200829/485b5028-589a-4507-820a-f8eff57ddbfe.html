Step 1. The beginning
You're just relaxing in a trade server, hoping for someone to buy your Killstreak Cow Mangler 6000. You have less than $5, and you want to be rich like those $$$$ing heavies, with their glitched unusual All-Father cosmetic and vintage Max's head. Suddenly, you just saw the thing of your life. A Strange Festive Minigun.


(Sorry for bad image quality)
It's.... beautiful. You really, really want it, so you use the glitchy !pc command. But wait a second, it's more than 22 $$$$ing keys!!! Surely, you CAN'T afford that, right?

HA HA HA


YES YOU CAN

JUST FOLLOW MY STEPS
Step 2. How to get started
Ok, the steps are pretty easy. Ever since the new update, people are loving the new use of... Nevermind that, let's focus on the minigun first!

1. Go to the community market - http://steamcommunity.com/market/

2. Click on 'Advanced Options' under Search


3. Find Team Fortress 2 in the list


4. Click search

Now, all you have to do is to search 'minigun'! Click on 'PRICE' -

WAIT!

We forgot the strange addition!

1. Go back to advanced options

2. Look for Strange in 'Quality' list, and tick the box.


3. Click search (after you have typed 'minigun')

Now you should see strange miniguns! There are lots of cool miniguns shown, but let's focus on getting the cheap one!

Click the 'PRICE' button on the top of the items list on the right. You should see the cheapest miniguns!


Now, this step is your choice. Choose any strange SKINNED minigun that is cheap - SKINNED minigun, NOT regular OR other types of miniguns! Personally, I like the King of the Jungle minigun. Let's say this is the one you want:


(To select an item from the list, just click on it!) Buy one from the following offers, and read the next step!
Step 3. The Festive Potion
After you have bought your preferred strange SKIINED minigun, you are (maybe) almost done! If you haven't got your minigun, GO GET IT! Now, about the festive-

'N3DD! YOU LIED! I NO SEE FESTIVE!!!!'

Relax. It's coming. All you have to do is to get this magicial thing:


What is it? It is a Festivizer (short for The Gun Mettle and Tough Break Festivizer). It allows you to put Festive mode on any skinned weapons. Scattergun, Grenade Launcher, Sniper Rife, basic stock weapons (some). This also means it would work on a skinned minigun.

Now, since the minigun is a strange quality, and is skinned, it may not be worth much (hopefully), and is quite dull. However, since I do not own a Festivizer, I will show you a video of how to attach Festive mode on your weapon.

But first, we must get the Festivizer. Here is the link to the Festivizer's listing in Community Market:

Festivizer

After you have bought one, open up your TF2 game, and you should get the Festivizer. Now, please watch this video to learn how to attach the Festivizer to your weapon (how many times have I said the word 'Festivizer'?)


(Special thanks to CherryTF for the video!)

Now you know how to use the Festivizer, go and use it on your strange skinned minigun!
Step 4. You're done, I guess?
If you successfully attached your Festivizer, you are done! You have hacked the TF2 ecomony!

What?


No, look at the minigun. It's a Strange Festive Minigun, only with a skin with it.
